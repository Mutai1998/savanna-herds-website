Company Overview

Savanna Herds Limited

Savanna Herds Ltd is a Kenyan agribusiness company, founded and officially registered in 2025, that focuses on the ethical sourcing and export of top-quality livestock alongside premium chilled and frozen meat products. The company blends commercial trade with sustainable agriculture and community development, positioning itself as a trusted partner in both local and international markets.

Our Impacts

Social Impact

Empowering Farmers

At SAVANNA, our impact is rooted in transforming the livelihoods of pastoral farmers through fair and reliable goat trade. By purchasing directly from local farmers, we create steady market access that boosts income, strengthens financial stability, and promotes sustainable growth. We prioritize the empowerment of women and youth by equipping them with skills, knowledge, and market exposure to lead in livestock management and trade. This inclusive approach uplifts households, builds resilience, and drives lasting progress within pastoral communities.

Supporting Communities

Our work goes beyond livestock trade — it strengthens entire communities.Through fair market access, capacity building, and sustainable partnerships, SAVANNA has helped pastoral families improve their livelihoods, increase household income, and secure better futures. By empowering women and youth, we are building confident, self-reliant communities that thrive economically while preserving their pastoral heritage and way of life.
